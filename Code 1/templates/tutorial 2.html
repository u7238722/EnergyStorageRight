Tutorial

This page is a simple tour which can guide you how to use our map

This is the base page of the world map, click the "change base map" button to change base map
In the change layer part, you can choose which energy layer to display on the map (they are stackable)
on the left top side, you can zoom and search a specific area on the map
In the select area part, you can use the single point selection to select a specific point and show the information
In the select area part, you can use the polygon selection to select a specific area and show the information, double click to finish selection
After selecting an area, click the "panel control board" button to go to the panel board page
In the panel board page, the ultimate layout pattern will auto fill the selected area. You can still manually add panel A or B, delete any panel, delete all panels, or add them to the report
You can use the remove selection to remove any selected point or area
In the choose energy part, you can choose any energy type to see the chosen energy data of your selected area
In the calculation area, you can configure the variables and press the "calculate" button for the Est. Investment, ROI and IRR results
You can press the "apply loan" button to get the bank information, click any bank to go to their page
You can press the "download" button to down the configuration and calculation information
